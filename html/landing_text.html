This tool was designed for Massachusetts residents who rely on a public water system (PWS) for their drinking water. Public water systems are required to test for regulated contaminants on a regular basis and provide those results to their customers. The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) sets legally enforceable limits on over 90 contaminants to protect public health.


This tool includes contaminants identified by EPA to increase the risk of cancer for long-term exposure above the Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL).
