EPA:

Environmental Protection Agency - The EPA is a United States (US) government agency that aims to create policy, conduct research, and implement interventions to maintain the health and safety of those living in the US and the environment that surrounds it.


PWS:

Public Water System. The system of organizations that work in conjunction with government groups, local communities, tribes, engineers and water operators to provide drinking water to the public in the US.


MCL:

Maximum Contaminant Level - The maximum contaminant level that is deemed “allowable” by the US EPA for contamination levels in drinking water for human consumption.


Increased Risk of Cancer:

31 of the contaminants on the EPA’s list of regulated contaminants are labeled with this Increased risk of cancer as a potential health effect of long-term exposure above the MCL. The chemicals in this resource are among these 31 contaminants with available data from the state of Massachusetts.
